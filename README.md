# MP3-Gotchi-v2
A pocket-sized offline MP3 player with a Tamagotchi-style monkey companion, built from scratch around a custom ESP32-S3 PCB.

**First version:**
![](https://github.com/Artisfera/MP3-Gotchi/blob/main/hardware/3dmodels/0.3.0/MP3-Gotchi-0.3.0.png)

## Why?
Have you ever been on a trip, lost signal again, and ended up sitting there with silence in your headphones, because playing MP3s on a phone just doesn’t feel the same?

My girlfriend has exactly that problem, so for our anniversary I’m planning to build her a one-of-a-kind Tamagotchi-style MP3 player. While listening to music, she’ll also be able to feed and take care of a little monkey pet. She loves monkeys, so it fits perfectly ✨
